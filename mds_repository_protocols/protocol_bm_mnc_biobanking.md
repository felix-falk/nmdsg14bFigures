# Precautions
- Never vortex cells
- Perform in accordance with BSL2
- Review risk assessment related to Trypan Blue and Turk

# Protocol
1. BM is diluted at the clinic in 3 ml RPMI1640 with 10 % FBS and 0.05 ml Heparine.
2. Note ml BM received
3. Into 3 ml Lymphoprep aliquot tubes marked with patient ID, *slowly* pipette down 3-4 ml BM, careful not to mix the two.
4. Centrifuge (400 g, 30 min, RT, acc 8, dec 3).
5. Carefully aspirate the MNC layer and add into 2 x 15 ml falcon tubes.
6. Wash with 5 ml wash media (dPBS + 10% FBS), centrifuge (300 g, 10 min, RT, 9 acc, 9 dec).
7. Pour out the wash media, flick to mix and then add another 5 ml wash media on top, centrifuge for another 10 minutes.
8. Dissolve MNCs in 5 ml wash buffer and pool in one 15 ml falcon tube.
9. Calculate MNCs per ml, total number of MNCs and % viability, with Turk (380μL + 20μL) or Trypan blue if sample is more than 24h old. Cells per mL = cells per large square * 20 (dilution factor) * 10 000 (volume factor).
10. Centrifuge (300 g, 10 min, RT, 9 acc, 9 dec).
11. Create aliquots according to table below. Dissolve correct number of MNCs in freezing media 1 (80% RPMI, 20% FBS), then add freezing media 1 for a total of 1000 μl freezing media 1 + MNCs in each cryovial. Then, add 1000 μL freezing media 2 (80% FBS, 20% DMSO) to each cryovial and immediately put the vials into a foam freezing chamber in a -80°C fridge over night.
12. After 24h in -80°C fridge, move the cells to -140°C biobank.

| **# BM MNCs** | **Aliquots**                                |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| ≤ 15 M        | 3 x 5 M                                     |
| 15 – 20 M     | 2 x 5 M and 1 x 10 M                        |
| 20 – 100 M    | 2 x 5 M, 2 x 10 M, 3 x 20 M                 |
| > 100 M       | 2 x 5 M, 2 x 10 M, 10 x 20 M, the rest 50 M |
