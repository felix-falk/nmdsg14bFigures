# Protocol
1. In Solna, retrieve the biobank vials of interest and put immediately in dry ice, in a plastic ice bucket container with lid.
2. Transport samples to Flemingsberg by car (do not use Pendelbuss or KI sample transport service).
3. If the vital frozen cells are to be used the following morning, put samples in -80C, otherwise put samples in -140C.
